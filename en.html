LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park

410 East Roda, Yangpu District

Huangxing Sports Park is the biggest golf driving range in China with close to 300 bays and 50 private rooms. The Guinness World Record Shanghai has awarded Huangxing Sports Park as the 'Driving Range with Most Numbers of Simultaneous Drivers" title. The park also houses most golf equipment commercial brands catering to all the golfers' need with golf retail area and golf equipment outlet.

LL Sport Plus Minhang Cultural Park

2088 Wuzhong Road, Minhang District, Shanghai

Created the first PPP comprehensive sports property. Completed phase one Sport Plus Top Soccer Park is a successful JV between Top Soccer and LL Sport Plus. It brings together traditional media and internet technology media combing the benefits of offline and online media. This is a break-thru in commercial sports chain brand name.

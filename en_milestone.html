2011.4.30

Grand Opening of the LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park.

2011.12.9

The first LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Membership Cup competition was held at Taicang East Sea Golf Club. More than 100 golfers attended.

2012.5.27

The first Inchcape All citizens Sports Festival was held at LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park and attracted 300 simultaneous golfers. The event was attended by various city and district high level officials as well as executives from city and national sports administrative department and LL Land Group. The event attracted wide media coverage from more than 20 mainstream media. The official Guinness World Record awarded LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park " The Biggest Driving Range that can house most number of Simultaneous Golfers" title.

2012.11.25

LL Land Sports Huangxing Driving Range was awarded "Best Business Research Value" title by the China Business News.

2012.12.5

LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Driving Range to become official practice field for the Shanghai University of Sport.

2013.8.9

Kobe Bryant visited LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park for the Nike Summer Tournament. Thousands of fans showed up to greet the international stardust.

2014.7.7

Chris Paul of the JORDAN FLIGHT TOUR together with Taiwanese celebrity Will Pan held an popular on ground event at the LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park.

2015.4.16

Giovanni Moreno, the soccer celebrity, the Head of Team Shanghai Greenland Shenhua FC visited LL Land Sport Plus Huangxing Sports Park for a TV show with the GREAT SPORTS channel and interacted with hundred of fans who showed up.

2016.2.28

LL Sport Plus begins relationship with the biggest local sports TV Channel Top Soccer (Shanghai Media Group) to operate Sport Plus Top Soccer Park. A Grand Opening Ceremony was held at the park with an exhibition match between six soccer teams attracting wide media coverage.

2016.4.18

The first episode of TV program "Sport Plus Top Soccer" launched on Great Sports TV channel. This weekly program quickly became one of the most populated sports program among mass audiences.

2016.7.10

The second annual Shanghai Citizen Sports Game Event soccer match was held at Sport Plus Top Soccer Park and made several Shanghai's first. This is a match with five highest level judges. The live TV broadcast lasted 2 hours which is the longest among amateur sports. The amateur sport event was attended by the highest local official Chief Huang from Shanghai Sports Administration. Chief Huang lead a team of city officials to watch the entire game and presented the final awards.

2016.11.27

Sport Plus together with BESTV, IPTV to organize the first ever Shanghai IPTV Soccer Cup which is a eight player soccer match. The final prizes amount to 300,000 RMB which is a record in the amateur soccer sport.

2016.12.8

In celebration of the 10 years anniversary of the Top Soccer Program a special event and TV program was held at the Sport Plus TOP Soccer Park. Shanghai Vice Mayor Sunwen together with Sport Administration Chief Huang attended and congratulated the event with thirty other city officials. International soccer celebrity Ryan Giggs, Gary Alexander Neville and other local and foreign sports stars was present at the event to celebrate with hundred of soccer fans.

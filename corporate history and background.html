A scientist of Shanxi Lutu, Professor zhao liangqi, of the Microbiology Department of the Shanxi University, in collaboration with a senior staff of Shanxi Lu, Mr. Mu Yaogui, who graduated from Shanxi Agriculuture bio-fertilizers from locally available mineral shale which originally cannot be used as fertilizers. They arrived at a mixture of various strains of microorganisms and invented a new product of mineral-based bio-fertilizers (the "MBF").

Shanxi Lutu currently holds a patent in PRC on the production method of the MBF produced (Patent number:ZL2009100737305.5) and hence possensses the microbial technology to manufacture MBF directly from mineral shale.

In 2011, Shanxi lutu purchased s piece of industrial land which is 34,256 square meters to epand its production capacity. In the same year, an approval to construct a 500,000 tons per annum of fertilizers production was granted by the Chinese Government. After several rounds of croporate transactions and fundraising, Shanxi Lutu is now a WFOE ultimately owned by share holders based in HK with the current share holding structure as described in the following section.At present,Shanxi Lutu has a production capacity of 100,000 tons perannum.
